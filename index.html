About the Photographer
I chase light and quiet moments — the ones that exist between breaths, between places.

Based in China. Shooting landscapes, architecture, and the spaces where nature meets human intention. I work with both digital and film, believing each has its own way of seeing.

Primary — Fujifilm X-T5 + 16-55mm f/2.8
Film — Nikon FM2 + Portra 400
Travel — Ricoh GR IIIx
Edit — Lightroom + Capture One
